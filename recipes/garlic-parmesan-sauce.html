Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce

Description

Garlic Parmesan Sauce is a creamy, dense sauce made from garlic, flour, milk, and cheese.

This is a versitile sauce that can be used for chicken wings, pastas and salmons

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic (Minced)
  • 2 tablespoon All Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Full Fat Milk
  • 1 cup Chicken Broth (Use Veg Broth for Veg Version)
  • ½ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon Freshly Cracked Black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and fry for a few seconds.
  3. Add flour and fry for a minute on low heat.
  4. Add milk gradually and keep whisking continuously to make a lump free mixture.
  5. Add chicken broth and cook until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt and black pepper and cook until cheese melts.
  7. Use the sauce as required.
